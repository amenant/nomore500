Suhoshin - Visual Novel

The story takes place at the beginning of the Joseon era, in a small village located to the south east. The player controls Yuri, a young man that recently became a guard. After a long period of absence he is back home, but a few dramatic events disturb the tranquility of the village. Yuri is asked to investigate, but quickly finds out that the problem is bigger than he initially thought.
