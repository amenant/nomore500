Story

Yuri is a young man that recently became an officer. After a long period of absence he is back home, but a few dramatic events disturb the tranquility of the village: people are getting killed mysteriously. Yuri is asked to investigate but quickly finds out that the problem is bigger than he initially thought. From his choices will depend the story!
